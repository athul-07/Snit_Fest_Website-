Rules:

1. This is a team event.
2. Each team should comprise minimum of 5 and maximum of 20 members.
3. Each participant should carry their institution id card along with them.
4. The song selection is open to the participants.
5. MP3 format of the music should be submitted to the stage committee immediately after entering the college premises.
6. Participants should wear proper dress and makeup.
7. Green rooms will be provided for the participants.
8. Each team will get maximum 15 minutes and minimum 10 minutes for their performance.
9. Properties are allowed if any and they should be mentioned during registration.
10. The participating team should report at campus an hour prior to the event.
11. The judgment will be based on choreography, costume and overall Presentation.
12. The decision of the judges will be final.

E certificates will be provided to the participants.
